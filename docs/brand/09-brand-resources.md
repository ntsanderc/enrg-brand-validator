## Personal Branding

**Can I create personal branding while still using the ENRG logo?**

Absolutely. Personal branding is encouraged, but all marketing must include the ENRG Realty logo and DRE number if required for compliance with your state's regulations.

---

## Testimonials and Client Content

**Can I use testimonials or client photos without written consent?**

- If your client writes you a review on your Google or Business Page, you may use that as content without consent.
- If your client sends you a text expressing appreciation for your work, you may use a screenshot or recreate this information into a graphic but must ask for permission to use their name. Otherwise, please black out their name.
- You must always ask permission before using a client photo.

## Understanding Lock Types

MAXA uses color-coded locks to indicate what you can and cannot edit in templates:

| Lock Color | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| **Gray** | Elements gently locked but user can modify |
| **Blue** | Text anchored in place (edit content, cannot move) |
| **Purple** | Edit, move, resize allowed, but cannot delete |
| **Yellow** | Reminder to update placeholder text |

# Social Media Strategy

## Facebook

**Best for:** Community building, promoting local events, long-form posts, and detailed real estate listings.

**Best practices:**
- Use high-quality photos or videos of listings and neighborhoods
- Share local news, events, and tips to foster community engagement
- Use Facebook Events for open houses or team-hosted activities
- Leverage Facebook Ads for targeted reach (especially geo-targeted)

**Ideal post ratio:**
- 60% lifestyle, community, and local highlights
- 25% listings and business updates
- 15% testimonials, agent spotlights, and behind-the-scenes content

---

## Instagram

**Best for:** Visual storytelling, branding, listing highlights, Reels, and engagement through Stories.

**Best practices:**
- Maintain a visually cohesive grid with brand-consistent tones
- Use Reels for quick tours, tips, or trends with engaging audio
- Post carousels for before-and-after transformations or multi-photo tours
- Leverage Stories for polls, questions, countdowns, or quick updates
- Use location tags and relevant real estate or local hashtags

**Ideal post ratio:**
- 50% brand/lifestyle content
- 30% educational content, market updates, and tips
- 20% personal brand building

---

## LinkedIn

**Best for:** Professional credibility, team announcements, thought leadership, and company culture.

**Best practices:**
- Share behind-the-scenes business strategy or market insights
- Post about team wins, awards, speaking engagements, and milestones
- Repurpose blog posts into thought-leadership summaries
- Engage with industry news to stay visible in real estate circles

**Ideal post ratio:**
- 40% thought leadership and articles
- 30% company culture and employee highlights
- 30% business updates or achievements

---

## YouTube

**Best for:** Long-form video content, virtual tours, interviews, and informative series about the buying or selling process.

**Best practices:**
- Use strong thumbnail images and keyword-optimized titles
- Break videos into chapters for ease of navigation
- Include a strong hook within the first 10 seconds
- Create playlists to organize content by theme
- Optimize descriptions and use end screens for cross-promotion

**Ideal post ratio:**
- 40% educational series or expert interviews
- 30% listing and virtual tours
- 30% lifestyle and community spotlight videos

---

## TikTok

**Best for:** Short-form video with entertainment value, viral trends, and quick educational content.

**Best practices:**
- Keep videos under 60 seconds unless the story requires more
- Use trending sounds while aligning with real estate topics
- Post home tours, fun agent moments, tips for buyers or sellers, or neighborhood guides
- Be authentic and casual—TikTok favors relatable over polished
- Engage in comments and duets to boost visibility

**Ideal post ratio:**
- 40% educational and real estate tips
- 30% entertaining or humorous agent content
- 30% property highlights or lifestyle storytelling
